Laban sport club indoor stadium

Laban Sports Club, the oldest of its kind in Meghalaya, had its new indoor stadium inaugurated in Shillong
Built at a cost of over Rs 2 crore, the new facility is purportedly the largest indoor stadium in the state - it has provision for 4 badminton courts in case you're wondering about the scale.
Local MLA Sanbor Shullai, local MDC Ricky Shullai and Sports & Youth Affairs director Matsiewdor War Nongbri were among the dignitaries.
Cultural programs were also held during the event
